🔒 How this app keeps your account safe

Every AI request is sent from this app to VibeLens's own server, which holds the OpenAI key and talks to OpenAI on your behalf — your device never sees that key, so it can't be extracted from the app. Your credit balance lives in our database (Firestore) and can only be changed by that same server, so the app itself has no way to grant free credits. Signing in uses Firebase Authentication (Google or email/password); we never see or store your password.
